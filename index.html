Historic places in the V&A Waterfront:

Here are some facts about the clock tower:

  • Situated near the site of the original Bertie's Landing Restaurant.
  • This was the original Port Captain's Office completed in 1882.
  • Restoration of the Clock Tower was completed towards the end of 1997.

Here are some facts about Rocket Shed

  • One of the buildings of the original harbour at the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront.
  • The shed is currently used as an information centre in the Waterfront

Swing Bridge

  • The bridge swings over the link between the Alfred Basin and the Victoria Basin in the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront.
  • It was designed and constructed in 1997.
